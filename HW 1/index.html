Liverpool F.C.

Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club in Liverpool, England, that competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football.

Main information

  • Full name: Liverpool Football Club
  • Nickname(s): The Reds
  • Founded: 3 June 1892
  • Ground: Anfield
  • Manager: Jürgen Klopp
